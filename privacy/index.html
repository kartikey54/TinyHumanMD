1. Scope

This policy applies to TinyHumanMD pages and tools served from the TinyHumanMD domain. It does not apply to external websites or services not controlled by TinyHumanMD.

2. Data We Process

Depending on configuration and usage, TinyHumanMD may process:

  • Page and feature usage events.
  • Technical metadata such as browser, device, operating system, and performance metrics.
  • Approximate or network-derived location and traffic metadata tied to IP/network characteristics.
  • Error and stability information for debugging and quality monitoring.

3. Local Browser Storage

Some calculators store values locally in your browser (for example, through local storage or IndexedDB) to improve usability and persistence on your device.

You control this local data by clearing browser storage on your device.

4. How Data Is Used

Processed data may be used to:

  • Operate and maintain site features.
  • Understand feature usage and improve clinical tools.
  • Monitor uptime, performance, and errors.
  • Protect against abuse and service misuse.

5. Sharing & Service Providers

Data may be shared with infrastructure and service providers that support site operation. TinyHumanMD does not sell personal data for consumer advertising purposes as part of normal site operations.

6. Retention

Retention periods vary by provider and local browser settings. Locally stored data remains on your device until you clear it. Third-party retention is governed by provider account settings and applicable law.

7. Security

TinyHumanMD uses reasonable safeguards for a public web service, but no internet system can guarantee complete security or uninterrupted availability.

Do not treat TinyHumanMD as a replacement for your institution's regulated systems, security controls, or privacy governance program.

8. HIPAA / BAA Position

TinyHumanMD is not represented as a HIPAA-covered clinical record platform and does not create a Business Associate Agreement through normal website access.

9. User Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, portability, or objection. Requests may require identity and authority verification.

You can also limit certain browser-based tracking through browser settings, extension controls, and local storage/cookie management.

10. Children

TinyHumanMD is intended for trained adults and is not directed to children for independent use.

11. International Use

If you access TinyHumanMD from multiple jurisdictions, you are responsible for complying with local privacy and data transfer requirements applicable to your use.

12. Policy Changes

This policy may be revised at any time. Updated versions are effective when posted.
