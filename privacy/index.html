TinyHumanMD is designed as a clinical decision-support reference site. It is not a healthcare delivery platform, not an electronic health record, and not a patient portal. This policy is intentionally explicit and risk-focused. It describes our intended data posture, your responsibilities, and legal boundaries around use of this website.
